<objective>
Add three safety features to the Resume editor: a confirmation step before deleting an experience/project/education entry or bullet, a visible Saved/Unsaved-changes indicator near the Save button, and a read-only preview of the current (including unsaved) resume state. Closes GitHub #6.

Purpose: Users can currently delete resume content with a single misclick and no undo, and have no visual signal of whether their edits are saved before navigating away.
Output: `Resume.jsx` gains `window.confirm` guards on all five destructive actions, a dirty-state indicator, and a Preview modal reusing the existing modal shell and read-only rendering conventions.
</objective>
